Detailed Description

Thresholds corresponding to Identifiers::ActionableQualityFeedback.

Comparing the values of Identifiers::ActionableQualityFeedback with Thresholds::ActionableQualityFeedback may indicate a corrective action for subsequent captures of the same subject.
